Dr.B.R.Ambedkar

Dr. B. R. Ambedkar was a great social reformer, economist, and the chief architect of the Indian Constitution. He fought against social discrimination and worked for the upliftment of the marginalized communities. He was also India's first Law Minister.

Periyar E.V. Ramasamy

Periyar E. V. Ramasamy was a prominent social reformer and rationalist from Tamil Nadu. He fought against caste discrimination, superstitions, and promoted self-respect and women's rights. He founded the Self-Respect Movement and Dravidar Kazhagam to fight for social justice.
